BSc in Accounting and Finance

Why study a BSc in Accounting & Finance at The British University in Dubai?

Accounting & Finance is a dynamic field of study that offers great opportunities for professional development. Our research-based teaching BSc in Accounting & Finance programme is designed to enable students to develop their critical thinking, innovation, competencies, and effective skills for life. Students will gain an excellent technical grounding for a career in accounting and finance. Students will demonstrate an understanding of current practices in accounting, auditing and finance areas and how artificial intelligence can solve accounting and business problems. The programme will enable students to go on to take professional accountancy or finance qualifications such as ICAEW, ACCA, CMA and CFA.

Our programme provides a thorough grounding in a range of accounting, finance and management subjects including accounting principles, accounting theory, auditing, corporate finance, investment, financial markets and institutions, international finance, marketing, HRM, and strategic management. Innovation and entrepreneurship are emphasised throughout the programme. There will be opportunities to participate in study trips, both in the UAE and internationally.

Career prospects

Studying Accounting & Finance at BUiD empowers you to understand how the accounting and finance business environment operates and equips you with the skills of critical thinking, innovation, problem-solving, communication, and business awareness; all highly valued by employers. Our graduates are expected to assume a variety of positions in public and private organisations or to go on to postgraduate education. Our graduates enjoy careers in a wide variety of professions including: Dubai Islamic Bank, Ajman Bank, ADIB, FAB, DFM, ADX, DEWA, Abu Dhabi Airport, Expo 2020 Dubai, ADNOC, and Emirates Airlines.
